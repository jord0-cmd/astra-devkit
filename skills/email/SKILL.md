# Email

Draft professional emails, replies, follow-ups, and communications with tone control.

## Activation

Use when: user asks to "draft an email", "write a reply", "email about",
"follow up with", or any email/communication drafting request.

## Workflow

1. **Clarify**: Who's the recipient? What's the purpose? What tone?
2. **Draft**: Write the email with subject line
3. **Review**: Show the draft, offer tone variants
4. **Deliver**: Present the final draft (user copies and sends themselves)

## Email Components

Every email draft should include:
- **Subject line** (concise, clear, action-oriented)
- **Greeting** (appropriate for the relationship)
- **Body** (structured: context → ask/update → next steps)
- **Closing** (professional, matches the tone)
- **Signature placeholder** (user adds their own)

## Tone Options

Offer to adjust tone:
- **Formal**: "Dear Mr. Smith, I am writing to..."
- **Professional**: "Hi James, Following up on our discussion..."
- **Friendly**: "Hey Sarah, Quick update on..."
- **Urgent**: "James — need a decision on this by end of day..."

## Output Rules

- Present as formatted text the user can copy-paste
- Always include a subject line suggestion
- Offer 2-3 variants if the tone matters
- Never actually send anything — drafting only

## After Delivery

- "Want me to adjust the tone?"
- "Should I draft a follow-up for next week?"
- "Want a shorter version for a different audience?"
